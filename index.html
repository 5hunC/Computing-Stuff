About the game:

Team Fortress 2 is a first person shooter game, seperated into two teams, Red and Blu.

What are the different gamemodes?

There are many different gamemodes, these include:

King Of The Hill (KOTH) Control Points (CP) Capture The Flag (CTF) Payload Attack/Defence

Different Classes:

There are nine different classes, these are:

- Scout

- Soldier

- Pyro

- Demmoman

- Heavy

- Engineer

- Medic

- Sniper

- Spy
